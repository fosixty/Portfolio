// 03 — Process

SIGNAL CHAIN

Input

Session prep starts with the artist, the record, and the goal of the performance so every technical choice serves the song.

Tracking

Tracking stays performance-focused: clean gain staging, efficient session flow, and a recording environment that helps artists stay locked in.

Processing

Mixing is detail-oriented and intentional, with an emphasis on vocal presence, balance, and keeping the emotional center of the record intact.

Output

Final delivery is organized, client-ready, and built for repeat listening across streaming, studio, and everyday playback systems.

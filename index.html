// 01 — Profile

WHO I AM

My background is rooted in audio engineering, with six years of hands-on experience in recording, mixing, production, and client-directed sessions. That foundation shaped the way I work across disciplines: detail-oriented, performance-focused, and committed to getting the strongest result out of every project.

I own and manage a recording studio in Marietta, where I oversee sessions, maintain workflows, and work directly with artists across pop, R&B, rap, hip-hop, rock, indie, alternative, and trap. I am detail-oriented, artist-focused, and especially dialed in on vocal engineering and capturing performances that feel finished.

Alongside audio, I have built a practical development workflow centered on HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Visual Studio Code, with tools like Git, Copilot, GPT, and Claude supporting research, implementation, debugging, and iteration. I use them as part of a hands-on process, not a substitute for knowing the work, and that technical discipline carries into everything I build and troubleshoot. This workflow allows me to move quickly while maintaining structure, performance, and reliability.
